Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a CSV or Excel file? +
Upload your CSV or Excel file using the Bulk Prediction section. Supported formats are CSV and XLSX.
How is prediction accuracy calculated? +
Predictions are generated using trained machine learning models based on student academic and behavioral data.
Can I download prediction results? +
Yes, after prediction you can download the generated results in CSV format.
Is my uploaded data secure? +
Yes, uploaded student data remains secure and private within the system.
What if prediction fails? +
Failed predictions usually occur because of missing or invalid values in uploaded files.
